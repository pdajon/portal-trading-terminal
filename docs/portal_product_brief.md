# Portal Product Brief

## What Portal is

Portal is a chart-first trading terminal built on Hyperliquid. The goal is to let traders plan and execute trades without leaving the charting environment.

## Target user

Portal is for discretionary crypto traders who currently analyze on TradingView and execute on Hyperliquid. They think in levels, ranges, trendlines, structure, and risk-to-reward.

## Product direction

- The chart is the app
- Execution should feel like an extension of planning
- Secondary information belongs in supporting panels
- The interface should feel minimal, premium, and focused

## MVP goal

Prove that planning and executing a Hyperliquid trade from a chart-first interface feels better than using TradingView and Hyperliquid separately.
